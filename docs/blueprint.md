# **App Name**: Engelsiz Dünya Platformu

## Core Features:

- Global Advocacy Hero Section: A prominent hero section featuring the slogan 'Herkes için erişilebilir bir dünya' and the message 'Dünya genelinde engelli hakları için çalışıyoruz', alongside a hero image and a 'Faaliyetlerim' (My Activities) button.
- Global Initiatives & Projects Overview: Showcase symbols and brief descriptions of global projects and collaborations (e.g., UN, international conferences) both within Turkey and worldwide, dynamically fetched.
- Segmented News & Announcements Module: Present recent news and announcements in a card-based format, categorized into 'Meclis'ten' (From Parliament), 'Saha Çalışmaları' (Fieldwork), and 'Uluslararası Görüşmeler' (International Meetings), with titles and dates, dynamically fetched from a database.
- WCAG-Compliant Accessibility Overlay Panel: A persistent panel on the left side of the interface, providing quick access to accessibility settings such as font size adjustments, contrast toggles, and dyslexia-friendly modes to comply with WCAG 2.1.
- Advocate Biography: 'Engellilerin Sesi': A dedicated section titled 'Engellilerin Sesi' (Voice of the Disabled) featuring a concise biography of the AK Party Member of Parliament and global disability rights advocate, elegantly designed for readability and impact.
- Interactive Citizen Contact Form: A modern, secure form allowing citizens to submit their name, email, and messages, with all submissions securely stored in a database.
- Activity Display Page: A dedicated section to list and detail the advocate's activities, with content dynamically loaded from a database, accessible via the 'My Activities' button.
- AI-Powered Message Intent Analysis Tool: Utilize generative AI as a tool to automatically categorize incoming messages from the contact form by their intent (e.g., 'Inquiry', 'Support Request', 'Suggestion'), streamlining response management.

## Style Guidelines:

- AK Party Blue (a deep, corporate blue, #003366) for branding, major headings, and primary interactive elements, conveying trust and professionalism.
- A vibrant orange (#FF6600) for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and key visual elements to draw attention.
- Clean white (#FFFFFF) for the main content areas, ensuring maximum readability and high contrast, especially for accessibility.
- 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly readable qualities, ensuring compliance with WCAG 2.1 accessibility standards for all content.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, minimalist sans-serif icons that align with the app's modern and professional aesthetic, designed with sufficient size and contrast for accessibility.
- Implement a fully responsive design compliant with WCAG 2.1 standards, ensuring optimal viewing and interaction across all devices. Layouts will feature clear content delineation, high contrast ratios, and easy navigation, including card-based designs for news and global initiatives.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transition animations for navigation and state changes, carefully designed to avoid distracting or disorienting users, maintaining WCAG 2.1 accessibility guidelines.
